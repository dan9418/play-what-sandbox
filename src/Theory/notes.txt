A concept is:

1) A keyCenter
    octave
    tonic
    accidental
2) An ordered set of intervals
    degree
    pitchClass
    octaveOffset
    direction

Concept operations: (independent)

1) Exact interval match
    Get note by noteIndex
2) Equivalent interval match
    Get note by pitchClass
3) keyCenter transposition
    Add or subtract an interval
4) Interval reversal
    Reverse order of intervals
5) Chord inversion
    Rotate intervals by x and shift an octave
6) Melodic inversion
    Flip direction of intervals
7) Get roman numeral (for diatonics)
    Get chord starting at degree x
    Ignores octaveOffsets
